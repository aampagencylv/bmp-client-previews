Pool Remodeling Services

Whether you need a simple resurface or a complete pool transformation, our team handles every aspect of the renovation:

  • Interior Resurfacing — Pebble Tec, quartz aggregate, or plaster finishes to replace worn, stained, or rough surfaces
  • Tile & Coping Replacement — Updated waterline tile, natural stone or precast coping, and decorative mosaics
  • Equipment Upgrades — Variable-speed pumps, energy-efficient heaters, salt chlorine generators, and smart automation systems
  • LED Lighting — Replace old incandescent lights with color-changing LED systems that use 80% less energy
  • Deck Resurfacing — Cool deck overlays, travertine pavers, stamped concrete, and Kool Deck refinishing
  • Feature Additions — Add waterfalls, fire bowls, tanning ledges, swim-up bars, or a spa that wasn't in the original design
  • Shape Modifications — Enlarge your pool, add depth, create a beach entry, or reconfigure the layout
  • Plumbing & Structural Repairs — Fix leaks, update plumbing lines, repair steel reinforcement, and address structural issues

Why Remodel Your Las Vegas Pool?

Pool remodeling isn't just cosmetic — it's one of the smartest home investments you can make in the Las Vegas Valley:

  • Increase home value — An updated pool can add $30,000–$50,000+ to your home's resale value
  • Cut energy costs — Modern pumps, heaters, and automation can reduce pool operating costs by 50–70%
  • Extend pool life — A quality resurface adds 15–20 years to your pool's lifespan
  • Improve safety — Updated drains, fencing, and non-slip surfaces meet current safety codes
  • Get the pool you actually want — Add the features you wish you'd had from day one

Signs Your Pool Needs Remodeling

Not sure if your pool needs a remodel? Here are the most common red flags we see in Las Vegas pools:

  • Rough or stained interior surface that tears up swimsuits and cuts feet
  • Cracked, loose, or missing waterline tile
  • Faded, chipped, or dated coping stones
  • Constantly running pump that spikes your electric bill
  • Persistent algae despite regular chemical treatment
  • Visible cracks in the pool shell or deck
  • Pool design that feels "stuck in the '90s"
  • Missing features you wish you had (spa, waterfall, lighting)

If any of these sound familiar, call us for a free assessment. We'll tell you exactly what's needed — and what's not — with no sales pressure.

Pool Remodeling FAQs

How much does pool remodeling cost in Las Vegas?

A basic resurface starts around $8,000–$12,000. A full remodel with new tile, coping, equipment, lighting, and feature additions typically ranges from $25,000–$80,000+. We provide detailed, itemized estimates so you know exactly where every dollar goes.

How long does a pool remodel take?

A basic resurface takes about 1–2 weeks. A comprehensive remodel with structural work and new features takes 3–6 weeks. We provide a specific timeline in your proposal and keep you updated throughout.

Can you remodel my pool in winter?

Yes — and it's actually the ideal time! Las Vegas winters are mild enough for pool construction, and you'll beat the spring rush. Your pool will be ready for the first warm weekend.

Can you add a spa to my existing pool?

Absolutely. Adding a spa is one of our most popular remodeling projects. We can build a connected spa with spillover into your pool, or a standalone spa nearby — depending on your yard layout and preferences.

Do I need permits for pool remodeling?

Some remodeling projects require permits (especially electrical, plumbing, or structural changes). We handle all permit requirements — you don't need to deal with Clark County yourself.
